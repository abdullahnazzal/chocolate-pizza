Chocolate Pizza

POSTED ON DEC 2013 / DESSERTS

It’s a chocolate covered chips bonanza! This “pizza & wings” combo is chips on chips. Start with a 16 ounce Chocolate Pizza that blends gourmet milk chocolate and crunchy, rippled potato chips. The secret is in the chocolate – we use only the highest grade Swiss-style milk chocolate to create a velvety smooth experience that sends your taste buds soaring. Sealed in cellophane and served in a custom pizza box. How do you like your chocolate covered potato chips? How about served in a Chocolate Pizza!

But we’re just getting started chocolate covered chips lover! Now, add a box of our famous Peanut Butter Wings. Peanut Butter Wings are crisp, rippled potato chips covered in creamy peanut butter and drenched in rich milk or dark chocolate. The Combo comes with an 8 ounce box but you can upgrade to a large, 16 ounce size for a few dollars more. Chocolate covered chips with peanut butter – insanely delicious!

Chocolate Pizza Company has been featured nationally on Food Network, Hallmark Channel, Discovery Channel, CNBC and more. Located in the rolling hills of Upstate New York, this family owned small business has been creating amazing gourmet chocolate specialties for over 30 years. Famous for their trademarked Chocolate Pizza & Peanut Butter Wings there is a chocolate specialty for every holiday or special occasion.

Allergy note: All our products are produced in a facility and on machines that share peanuts, tree nuts, eggs, wheat, milk and soy.
